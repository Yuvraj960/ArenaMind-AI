# Stadium Amenities

## Food & Drink

- **Food Court A** (Concourse Level 1, North): International cuisine, halal options
- **Food Court B** (Concourse Level 1, South): Local favorites
- **Express Kiosks**: Located at every gate, snacks and beverages
- **VIP Dining**: Level 3, advance booking required

All food outlets accept card and mobile payment. Cash available at info desks.

## Restrooms

- Every gate has restroom facilities
- Accessible restrooms at Gates A, C, D, F (clearly signed)
- Family restrooms at Gates A and D

## First Aid

- 4 medical stations: Gate A, Gate C, Gate F, and Central (Level 2)
- AED (defibrillator) units: see stadium map for locations
- Any staff member can call a medical team

## Information Desks

- Main desk: Gate A entrance
- Secondary desks: Gates C and F
- Open from 3 hours before kickoff until 1 hour after match

## Lost & Found

Report lost items at any information desk. After the match, call +1-800-ARENAMIND or email lostandfound@arenamind.ai
