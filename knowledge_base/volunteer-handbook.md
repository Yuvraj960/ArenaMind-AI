# Volunteer Quick Reference

## Your Role
- Welcome and assist fans with directions, information, and accessibility needs
- Report incidents immediately to your shift supervisor or Security Command
- Know the location of your assigned gate's facilities, exits, and medical station

## Key Numbers
- Security Command: Dial 911 or use red emergency button
- Medical: Gate A, Gate C, Gate F, Central (Level 2)
- Lost & Found: Main Info Desk (Gate A)
- Volunteer Coordinator: Channel 3 on radio

## When a Fan Asks for Directions
- Use the ArenaMind app or stadium map
- Gate F is the always-staffed accessible entrance
- Nearest restroom: see your gate's facility map
- Food courts: Level 1, North (Court A) and South (Court B)

## Accessibility Requests
- Wheelchair assistance: call Transport Services (Channel 5)
- Sign language interpreter: radio to Info Desk
- Visual assistance: describe the scene clearly and offer arm

## Escalation
If in doubt, escalate to your shift supervisor. Never guess medical procedures or security protocols.

## Lost Children
Direct to Volunteer Coordinator at Gate A immediately. Do not delay.

## Crowd Control
- Stay calm and smile
- Direct flow toward less-congested gates
- Never physically restrain a fan - call security for assistance
