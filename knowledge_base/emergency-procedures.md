# Emergency Standard Operating Procedures

## Medical Emergency

1. CALL: Dial 911 or activate stadium emergency system
2. Alert nearest staff/volunteer
3. Do NOT move the person unless in immediate danger
4. Clear a 2-meter perimeter
5. Direct medical team when they arrive

Nearest medical: Central Medical Center, Gate A, Gate F, and AED units throughout.

## Fire/Evacuation

1. STAY CALM - Follow staff instructions
2. Do NOT use elevators
3. Proceed to nearest indicated exit (green signs)
4. Assembly points: Car Park A (North), Car Park B (South)
5. Do not re-enter until all-clear given by Security Command

## Suspicious Object

1. DO NOT touch, move, or cover the object
2. Alert nearest security officer immediately
3. Move away to a safe distance
4. Note exact location and description for reports

## Overcrowding/Crowd Surge

1. Do not push - encourage calm movement
2. Staff will redirect flow to less-crowded gates
3. Follow spoken announcements
4. Move toward walls/wider areas, not the center of passages

## Communication

- Stadium PA announcements in English, Spanish, French, Arabic
- Red emergency buttons at every gate
- Direct line to Security Command: 911 or stadium emergency channel
