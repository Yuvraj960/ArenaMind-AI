# Frequently Asked Questions

## Can I leave and re-enter the stadium?
Yes, with a valid ticket. Your ticket will be scanned at exit and re-entry gates. No re-entry within the last 15 minutes before kickoff.

## Is there a bag policy?
Bags larger than A4 size must be checked at bag storage near Gate A. Small personal bags are permitted.

## Can I bring food and drinks?
No outside food or beverages. Sealed water bottles may be confiscated. Empty reusable water bottles are welcome - free water refill stations throughout.

## Where is the accessible seating?
Accessible seating is in Sections 102, 103, 118, 119 (lower bowl) and Sections 204-206, 220-222 (upper bowl).

## How do I find my seat?
Your ticket shows Section, Row, and Seat number. Stadium maps are at every gate and on the ArenaMind app.

## Is there WiFi?
Free stadium WiFi: Network "ArenaMind-2026", no password required.

## What happens if it rains?
The stadium is fully covered - no match cancellation due to rain. In severe weather (lightning), follow staff instructions for sheltered areas.

## Where is the prayer room?
Prayer rooms are on Concourse Level 2 (North) and Level 3 (South). Signs clearly marked.
