# Accessibility Services

## Wheelchair Access

All gates have wheelchair access. Gate F (Accessible Entrance, SW corner) is recommended as it has:
- Dedicated priority lane
- Staff assistance available 24/7
- Nearest accessible restrooms
- Accessible parking nearby

## Accessible Routes

- Elevators available at every gate
- Ramps instead of stairs on all main concourse routes
- Wide aisles in all food and merchandise areas
- Sign language interpreters at central information points

## For Blind/Low Vision Visitors

- Tactile paving throughout the stadium
- Audio describe service available on request at any information desk
- Large-print match programs available

## For Deaf/Hard of Hearing Visitors

- Real-time captioning on all main screens
- Sign language interpretation at key moments
- Visual alert systems in accessible restrooms

## Accessible Restrooms

Located near every gate and on every concourse level. Signs clearly marked with international accessibility symbols.

## Seating

Accessible seating sections are spread throughout the stadium at every price level. Contact your ticket provider for accessible seating requests.

## Assistance

Press the red assistance button at any gate or ask any staff member in a high-visibility vest for help.
